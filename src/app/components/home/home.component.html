How it works?

WeLink creates a safe, collaborative environment where people can have meaningful conversations with social workers and access personalised support from voluntary services in their community.

How we do this

Through an easy-to-use automated platform, we enable healthcare providers to move beyond the prescription pad and to address the wider social determinants of health. This creates a more holistic treatment paradigm for patients suffering from conditions like social isolation, as well as offering support to people caring for terminally ill partners or even those seeking financial aid. WeLink is an aggregator of holistic, non-medical services otherwise not readily accessible or unknown to them.
